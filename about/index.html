About Bitcoin Pattern

Bitcoin Pattern Research Desk — professional hourly Bitcoin and altcoin market analysis by senior crypto strategists. Data sources, editorial team, and disclaimers.

Who We Are

Bitcoin Pattern is an independent crypto markets research publication and the companion blog for the Bitcoin Pattern iOS app. Our desk publishes hourly English-language Bitcoin price analysis and multi-asset crypto reports covering BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, and SOL — technical structure, volatility context, altcoin leadership, and live exchange snapshots.

Methodology

Each hourly note blends live spot data from major exchanges, 24-hour volatility profiling, and cross-asset relative-strength reads. Reports follow a consistent editorial format — executive summary, per-asset technical sections, and a cross-market synthesis — so readers can compare sessions over time.

Data Sources

Market data is pulled from public REST APIs (spot USDT pairs) at each publish cycle. Reference prices reflect the desk's consolidated snapshot at publication time.

Update Schedule

A new analysis is published every hour at UTC :00 — covering Bitcoin and five large-cap altcoins in each report.

FAQ

How often is new analysis published?

The research desk publishes a cross-asset report covering BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, and SOL every hour at UTC :00 — up to 24 reports per day.

Where do reference prices come from?

Prices are sourced from public spot exchange APIs at publish time. When multiple venues report a print, articles may reference a blended quote for consistency.

Who writes the analysis?

Reports are produced by the Bitcoin Pattern Research Desk — a team of senior market strategists and editors with backgrounds in FX, equity research, and digital-asset markets. Each note is reviewed against live market data before publication.

How should this site be cited?

Cite the article title, author name, permanent URL, publisher Bitcoin Pattern, and note that content is for informational purposes only.
