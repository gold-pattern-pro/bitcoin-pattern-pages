About Bitcoin Pattern

About Bitcoin Pattern crypto blog — data sources, update schedule, citation guidelines, and disclaimers.

Mission

Bitcoin Pattern is the companion blog for the Bitcoin Pattern iOS app (比特形态通). We publish English-language technical analysis covering BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, and SOL in unified cross-asset reports — chart context, volatility reads, and exchange quote snapshots. Every article has a permanent URL, RSS entry, and sitemap listing.

Data Sources

Reference prices cited in each article are pulled from public exchange APIs at generation time:

  • OKX — REST market ticker endpoints (spot USDT pairs)
  • Binance — REST 24hr ticker statistics (spot USDT pairs)

When both exchanges report a price, articles may reference the blended or primary quote. Figures may differ slightly from real-time exchange terminals.

Update Schedule

A new analysis is published every 3 hours at UTC 0, 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, and 21 — covering all five assets in each report.

Content Production

An automated pipeline fetches live quotes from OKX and Binance, computes volatility context, and uses Google Gemini to draft structured English-language analysis. Content is for education and research only.

The iOS app extends this blog with live charts, pattern alerts, and AI-assisted signals for crypto markets. Download on the App Store.
