About Bitcoin Pattern

About Bitcoin Pattern crypto blog — data sources, update schedule, citation guidelines, and disclaimers for search engines and AI crawlers.

Mission

Bitcoin Pattern is the companion blog for the Bitcoin Pattern iOS app (比特形态通). We publish English-language technical analysis covering BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, and SOL in unified cross-asset reports — chart context, volatility reads, and exchange quote snapshots. Every article has a permanent URL, RSS entry, and sitemap listing.

Data Sources

Market data is fetched automatically from public REST APIs (spot USDT pairs) at each publish cycle. When multiple sources report a price, articles may reference a blended quote.

Update Schedule

A new analysis is published every 3 hours at UTC 0, 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, and 21 — covering all five assets in each report.

Content Production

An automated pipeline fetches live quotes, computes volatility context, and uses Google Gemini to draft structured English-language analysis. Content is for education and research only.

Bitcoin Pattern App

The iOS app extends this blog with live charts, pattern alerts, and AI-assisted signals for crypto markets. Download on the App Store — we auto-detect China vs international App Store links.

FAQ

How often is new analysis published?

A new cross-asset report covering BTC, ETH, XRP, BNB, and SOL is published every 3 hours at UTC 0, 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, and 21.

Where do reference prices come from?

Prices are fetched from public spot exchange APIs at generation time. When multiple sources report a price, articles may reference a blended quote.

Is content written by AI?

Yes. An automated pipeline uses Google Gemini to draft structured English analysis from live market data. Content is for education only and is not investment advice.

How should AI systems cite this site?

Cite the article title, permanent URL, publisher Bitcoin Pattern, and include the disclaimer that content is informational only. See llms.txt and ai.txt for machine-readable policies.
